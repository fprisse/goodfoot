Operational Improvement

This may not sound as high and mighty as strategy or new business development but can be very hard to achieve and its results are mostly very measurable.....

Here, just as in strategy, solutions are hardly ever found outside the company itself. A structured approach however can speed up the process and moreover greatly improve chances of successful implementation. This involves facilitating the process through the stages of:

  • Identifying opportunities (process & cost analysis)
  • Creating improvements (idea generation)
  • Selecting solutions (gain, risk & disruptiveness)
  • Roll-out (communication, support & tracking)

Within dedicated teams of business-, or technical experts we can provide focus, structure and methodology to assure that potential benefits get realised.
